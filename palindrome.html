Name: Is Palindrome

Description:

Test if a word is a palindrome. A word is a palindrome if and only if it can be read from left to right or from right to left: gag; kayak; php; radar;...

Instructions:

Breakdown of the processing: We compare the characters located at the ends of the word:

  • If they are equal, we test the rest of the word.
  • If they are different, we stop.

Stop condition: An empty word or a word containing a single character is a palindrome.
